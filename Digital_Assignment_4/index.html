That Dog that can't stop thinking about those ghosts he saw last night.

  • Originality: This game started as a retooling of homing stars, hence the JS filename, but became its own game after I started playing around with a few ideas and incorporating them into the theme.
  • Technical Merit: This game has a difficulty that scales with the score of the player, mostly original assets and 1 whole sound clip. I attempted to modulate its pitch but couldnt get javascript to cooperate.
  • Prototype Postmortem: What did you learn from this prototype?
      I got much better at producing and managing assets.
    What was the easiest or hardest part of making it?
      Easiest part was getting the base concept in play. I had a proto-proto type if you will, very early on. However, the small things ended up giving me the most trouble such as attempting to pitch modulate sound effects.
  • Prototype Assets: I borrowed the dog sprite OpenGameArt.ORG (though I created a few extra frames myself and stitched it together) , made the tiles and tile map myself, the sound clip is the dog note from mario paint, though I doubt it is the source of the sound bite.
